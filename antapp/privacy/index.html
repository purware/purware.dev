AntApp Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 May 2026

AntApp ("we", "the app") is a productivity app that runs entirely on your device. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

TL;DR

  • AntApp does not collect, transmit, or sell your personal information.
  • The app runs entirely on your device. We do not operate any backend server.
  • The only data that leaves your device is purchase information you submit to Google Play (on Android) or the App Store (on iOS) when you buy a paid tier of AntApp.

What data AntApp handles

All data AntApp creates or uses is stored only on your device, in the app's local sandbox. None of it is uploaded to any server we control. This generally includes:

  • Settings and configuration you enter into the app.
  • On-device state the app needs to function.
  • Premium entitlement state — whether you have an active paid subscription or lifetime purchase, and (where the platform reports it) when it expires.

AntApp does not collect:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, location, calendar, messages, or files.
  • Crash reports or analytics.
  • Any device identifier we control.

Permissions

AntApp requests only the operating-system permissions needed for its core functionality. Any data accessed via these permissions is processed entirely on-device and is never transmitted to us. See the app's store listing for the specific permissions it requests and why.

Purchases

Where AntApp offers an in-app purchase, payment is handled by Google Play Billing on Android or the App Store on iOS. When you make a purchase:

  • Google Play (or Apple) handles the payment. AntApp never sees your payment-card details.
  • The app receives a signed receipt from Google or Apple confirming the purchase, which is stored locally on your device to remember your entitlement.
  • Purchase data is governed by Google's privacy policy and, on iOS, Apple's privacy policy.

Contacting us

When you email us (e.g. at antapp@purware.dev), we receive whatever you include in your message — typically your email address, plus any details, attachments, or device info you choose to share. We keep this correspondence in our email inbox so we can reply and refer back to it later. We don't import it into the app or share it with third parties beyond our email provider.

Data sharing

We do not share your data with third parties. The only outbound data flow is the purchase request you initiate to Google Play or the App Store.

Data retention and deletion

Because AntApp stores all data locally:

  • Uninstalling the app removes all AntApp data from your device.
  • You can also clear data via your operating system's app settings.
  • There is no server-side data for us to delete because we do not operate a server.

Security

Your AntApp data is stored in the app's per-app sandbox, which the operating system isolates from other apps. We do not transmit your data over the network.

Children's privacy

AntApp is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top and (where reasonable) surface the change in the app.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email antapp@purware.dev.
