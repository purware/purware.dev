# purware Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 11 May 2026**

purware ("we") builds productivity apps that run entirely on your device. This policy explains what data our apps handle and how.

## TL;DR

- None of our apps collects, transmits, or sells your personal information.
- Our apps run entirely on your device. We do not operate any backend server.
- The only data that leaves your device is purchase information you submit to Google Play (on Android) or the App Store (on iOS) when you buy a paid tier of one of our apps.

## What data our apps handle

All data our apps create or use is stored **only on your device**, typically in a local database in the app's sandbox. None of it is uploaded to any server we control. The specific categories vary by app, but generally include:

- **Settings and configuration** you enter into the app.
- **On-device state** the app needs to function (for example, usage measurements computed locally).
- **Premium entitlement state** — whether you have an active paid subscription or lifetime purchase, and (where the platform reports it) when it expires.

We do not collect:

- Your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, location, calendar, messages, or files.
- Crash reports or analytics.
- Any device identifier we control.

## Permissions

Our apps request only the operating-system permissions needed for their core functionality. Any data accessed via these permissions is processed entirely on-device and is never transmitted to us. See each app's store listing for the specific permissions it requests and why.

## Purchases

Where an app offers an in-app purchase, payment is handled by Google Play Billing on Android or the App Store on iOS. When you make a purchase:

- Google Play (or Apple) handles the payment. Our apps never see your payment-card details.
- The app receives a signed receipt from Google or Apple confirming the purchase, which is stored locally on your device to remember your entitlement.
- Purchase data is governed by [Google's privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) and, on iOS, [Apple's privacy policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

## Data sharing

We do not share your data with third parties. The only outbound data flow is the purchase request you initiate to Google Play or the App Store.

## Data retention and deletion

Because our apps store all data locally:

- **Uninstalling an app** removes all of its data from your device.
- You can also clear data via your operating system's app settings.
- There is no server-side data for us to delete because we do not operate a server.

## Security

Your data is stored in each app's per-app sandbox, which the operating system isolates from other apps. We do not transmit your data over the network.

## Children's privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top and (where reasonable) surface the change in the affected app.
